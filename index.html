Welcome to My Portfolio

Hi, I’m Kajsa Granström, a passionate graphic designer specializing in branding, digital illustration, and user experience design. I believe in the power of design to tell stories, create connections, and leave lasting impressions.

My work blends creativity with strategy, ensuring every project is not only visually compelling but also meaningful and effective. Explore my portfolio to see how I bring ideas to life through bold visuals, thoughtful layouts, and innovative design solutions. Let’s create something amazing together!
